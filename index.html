Welcome to Rollerskate Spots Australia

A site dedicated to sharing skate spots for rollerskaters (and rollerbladers) in Australia



Submit a Skate Spot

Fill out the Google Form linked here to add a Skate Spot! The more detail and pictures you can provide the better!


Verify a Skate Spot

We rely on community verification to determine if the information submitted is correct. To verify a skate spot, navigate to that page and use the form linked or click here.


Update a Skate Spot

Is the information listed for your favourite skate spot incorrect or incomplete? Please let us know with the Google Form linked here so that we can make the appropriate ammendments.
