Suncorp Stadium

Address: 40 Castlemaine St, Milton QLD 4064

City: Milton, Brisbane // Yuggera

Information

Surrounding Suncorp Stadium is a large expanse of flat concrete. It's mostly flat and good for beginners. There are groups that meet up here on Saturday mornings, when games aren't on.

Verified: Yes

Slope: Flat

Surface: Smooth Concrete

Cracks: Small

Difficulty: Beginner
