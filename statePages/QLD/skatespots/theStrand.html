The Strand

Address: The Strand, North Ward, Townsville

City: North Ward, Townsville // Gurrambilbarra Wulgurukaba People

Information

A beautiful 2.5km esplanade with amazing views and cracks so small you can barely feel yourself rolling over them. Can get quite busy on weekends, so best to visit weekdays if you are a beginner.

Verified: No

Slope: Flat

Surface: Smooth Bikeway Concrete

Cracks: Small

Difficulty: Beginner
