Riverside Promenade

Address: 531 Seventeen Mile Rocks Rd, Seventeen Mile Rocks QLD 4073

City: Seventeen Mile Rocks, Brisbane // Yuggera

Information

Scenic route from Rocks Riverside Park to the Centenary Highway. The part along the park is rough gravelly road and the cement section begins at the end of Counihan Rd. The gaps between the sections of cement can get pretty bumpy and there's a section closer to the bridge which has new patches to fill in cracks. It may be tricky for beginners. There's some slopes which are easily walkable but can be difficult to climb on skates. There is a skatepark at the end.

Verified: Yes

Slope: Sloped sections

Surface: Smooth and rough Concrete

Cracks: Medium

Difficulty: Intermediate
