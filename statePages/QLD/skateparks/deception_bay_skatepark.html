Deception Bay Skatepark

Address: 100 Maine Terrace, Deception Bay QLD 4508

City: Deception Bay, Morton Bay // Yuggara

Information

Located near the Deception Bay PCYC and Aquatic Centre, this is a small beginner friendly skatepark. There are ramps for all levels at varying heights. There is no shade however and it is recommended you bring your own water bottle.

Surface: Skatepark Concrete

Features: Variety of ramp heights.

Verified: Yes
