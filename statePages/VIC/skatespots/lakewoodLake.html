Lakewood Lake

Address: Lakewood Dr, Knoxfield VIC 3180

City: Lakewood Lake, Knoxfield // Boonwurrung

Information

A lovely scenic path just over 1km circumference. Fairly smooth concrete but with a decent amount of twigs and whatnot, so definitely need some balance to skate here. Some nice little hills to go up and down, and nice views the whole way. Fun for cruising.

Verified: No

Slope: Small hills

Surface: Rough Concrete

Cracks: Small, Medium

Difficulty: Intermediate
