MAB Eastern Promenade

Address: MAB Eastern Promenade, Tonsley SA 5042

City: Adelaide, South Australia // Kaurna

Information

Sheltered public plaza with security guard, several different areas connected by open corridors. Available for skating in the evenings and weekends, but not during workdays as it is populated by hordes of students. Don't damage the furniture, if you must grind then bring your own rails. There's plenty of space, but on weekends watch out for scooters and families having picnics.

Verified: No

Slope: Mostly Flat

Surface: Smooth Concrete

Cracks: Small

Difficulty: Beginner
